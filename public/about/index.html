    About Dave Bettin

    I am married to my best friend and have three wonderful kids. Without any question, I am the happiest when we are spending time together as a family. We live in a beautiful mountain town in Oregon named Bend. Have you heard of Bend? if not, check it out.

    I am currently a co-founder at an early stage financial data software company. Over the past 14 years, I have worked on my fair share of software gigs; some of them I’ve enjoyed, others not so much. I hope to continue to do what I enjoy.

    Technically, I don’t prescribe to any religion or align with any dogma; I select the technologies that help me get stuff done. I love writing code in C#, Javascript and Node. I enjoy working on Web APIs, big data and solving those pesky type of problems.

    I continuously strive to find the simplicity in everything, however, I usually fail in doing so.
