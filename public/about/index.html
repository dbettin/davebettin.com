About Dave Bettin

Oct 17th, 2011

I am a family man. I am married to my best friend and have three wonderful mini Bettins. Without any question, I am the happiest when we are spending time together as a family. We live in a beautiful mountain town in Oregon named Bend. Have you heard of Bend? if not, check it out.

I also love being a geek, traveling, watching the Huskers, reading, drinking wine, screwing up my fantasy football lineups and being outdoors. I wish had more time to learn about other interesting things.

I am currently a co-founder at a very early stage financial data software company. Over the past 14 years, I have worked on my fair share of software gigs; some of them I’ve enjoyed, others not so much. I hope to continue to do what I enjoy.

Technically, I don’t prescribe to any religion or align with any dogma; I select the technologies that help me get shit done. I love writing code in C#, Io, Javascript and sometimes Ruby. I enjoy working on web api(s), big data and solving those pesky type of problems.

I continuously strive to find the simplicity in everything… I usually fail.

Questions/Comments? Please feel free to contact me.
